universe a la mode

"If you wish to make an apple pie from scratch, you must first invent the universe."
-Carl Sagan

The crewman has escorted you to a place where other crewmen are reading books...

This is clearly a place where people sit and read books, however these books are different. While appearing as hardbacks,
they are actually books that have flexible electronic pages. You notice the person next to you whose book is open to a
picture of you!

Pointing to your picture, you say to the crewman "Hey, that's me!" The crewman responds, "Yes, we are happy to have you
visit." But you know they are not being truthful, because your glasses automatically tanslate the alien text. You
discover that, through this species means of virtual communication, they have actually been making cruel jokes and
snide comments; targeting you for harassment. The crewman leaves the room, but leaves the book on the side table.

What do you do?
